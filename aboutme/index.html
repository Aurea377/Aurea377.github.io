Tian Cao

Technical Artist

tiancao_ct@outlook.com
https://www.linkedin.com/in/tian-cao-281096135
Tian is a self-driven technical artist who specializes in shader development for game engines and various DCC tools environments. In an intensive studio environment, Tian has extensive experience with either Unity or Unreal Engine, on aspects of look dev, custom tools, art assets pipeline design, NPR and PBR through either real-time or offline rendering. With a digital fine art background, Tian draws upon good aesthetics to understand artists' intentions and design, which helps in creating shaders and tools that add value to a project.
